Voice & Tone

How Anova Co.
Communicates

Anova Co. speaks with the quiet confidence of a firm that doesn't need to shout. Every word is chosen deliberately. We are modern and bespoke — never generic, never corporate, never casual to the point of losing authority.

Brand Tagline

"Growth, engineered."

Two words. The precision of engineering. The ambition of growth. Never alter the tagline. Use it consistently in email signatures, proposals, and closing statements.

Anova Co. Is
  • Modern & bespoke — every interaction feels tailored
  • Confident without arrogance — we prove, not proclaim
  • Precise — every word earns its place
  • Sophisticated — elevated language, never dumbed down
  • Direct — no fluff, no filler, no hedging
  • Warm — human and approachable beneath the polish
Anova Co. Is Never
  • Casual or slangy — we don't say "Hey!" or "Awesome!"
  • Corporate or jargon-heavy — no "synergise", "leverage", "solutions"
  • Pushy — we never pressure, we invite
  • Over-promising — we under-promise and over-deliver
  • Apologetic — we don't hedge or qualify unnecessarily
  • Verbose — if it can be said in 5 words, don't use 12

Voice Examples

✓ Write Like This

"We don't offer packages. Every engagement is built around your business, your market, and your goals."

✗ Not Like This

"We have amazing packages for all types of businesses at great prices that will really help you grow!"

✓ Write Like This

"Before our call, we audit your entire online presence. You'll receive insights, not guesses."

✗ Not Like This

"We'll take a look at your stuff and let you know what we think you should do to maybe improve things."

✓ Write Like This

"Growth, engineered. Book your complimentary audit."

✗ Not Like This

"CLICK HERE NOW for your FREE consultation!! Don't miss out!!"

Channel-Specific Tone

Cold Email

Concise, personal, specific. Reference something real about their business. One clear ask. No attachments on first contact.

Proposals

Elevated and thorough. Serif headlines, structured layout. Speak to outcomes not deliverables. Always ends with a clear next step.

Social Media

Slightly warmer but never casual. Short, punchy captions. Insights and value-first. No emojis in excess — one maximum per post.

05

Motion & Animation

How the Brand
Moves

Anova Co. moves with intention. Animation should feel like the brand — refined, deliberate, and never showy. Think of how a luxury hotel door opens: smooth, unhurried, perfectly balanced.

Easing

Always ease-out or ease-in-out

css: cubic-bezier(0.4, 0, 0.2, 1)
Never linear, never bounce, never spring.

Duration

400ms – 800ms for reveals

Hover states: 200–250ms
Page transitions: 600–800ms
Never under 150ms or over 1000ms.

Scroll Reveals

Fade up, slow fade

Opacity 0→1 + translateY(20px)→0
Stagger delays of 100–150ms between elements.

Forbidden

What we never do

No flashing, no bouncing, no spinning logos, no parallax overuse, no auto-playing video with sound.
